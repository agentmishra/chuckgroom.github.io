Consulting

Do you need help hiring engineers and leadership talent for your team?

Is there a business concept that you want to rapidly prototype and validate?

Are you trying to evaluate an existing body of software for strengths and weaknesses?

Are you at the early stage of your business, and need technical guidance and support to get started building your product?

Do you want to look at the "big picture" to make sure you have the right product and execution roadmap?

I offer three different models for working with companies.

  • Advisorship: a long-term relationship. We will meet for a few hours a month to discuss current challenges and strategy.
  • Consulting CTO: a project-based engagement for a particular problem or task. I have extensive experience helping teams with their recruiting pipeline and process, as well as design, coding, and due dilligence projects.
  • Fractional CTO: ongoing tasks need support right away. A team may not yet need a full-time CTO or cannot wait for a full-time hire. Often, this role includes helping to hire a replacement or to identifying ways to fill the gaps.
